How Does it Work?

Rec Bot uses Natural Language Processing and a Machine Learning AI to read not just your Chart of Accounts and Cost Centres, but your handwritten transaction descriptions. Interpreting meaning in your accounts, just like a human accountant.

Step One

Upload all transactions from the account you want to reconcile to Rec.Bot as a .csv file.

Step Two

Rec.Bot's Natural Language Processing AI scans your transactions and eliminates transactions that net to zero.

Step Three

Rec.Bot returns a list of all reconciled transactions, and a separate list of all cleared transactions.

Step Four

Relax as RecBot runs your balance sheet for you!
